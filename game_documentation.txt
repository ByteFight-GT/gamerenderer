GAME IDEA:
Grid-based game with player blue and player green
Stamina:
Stamina powerups randomly spawn like the apples we had last year (replenishes a certain amount of stamina)
Gain stamina every round - much lower
Gaining control of a hill (50%+ which is static, part of the map) increases the max stamina you have. Hills and max stamina can be lost.
Optional(90/10): You also naturally regenerate stamina according to how many squares adjacent to you are your color + Base amount B (anywhere from B + [0, 9], or if we do 2 square adjacency B + [0, 25]). 
You can take extra moves using stamina (first move takes none)
Movement steps
Regular steps (-1 layer of opponent paint)
Optional(50/50): Erase step (erases all layers at the cost of extra stamina)
Beacons step: You can place a beacon on the square you left behind (costs)
You can place a beacon if you have atleast Q squares in a  p x p are painted your color (e.g. if you have at least 15 squares painted in a 5x5)
It decreases all painted squares of your color in that p x p by 1, increases all painted squares of enemy color in that p x p by 1, then places a beacon at the center
Beacons prevent the opponent from painting a square, and count as your color
You may move between beacons as a standard movement step
Optional (70/30): Maybe moving between beacons should consume one of the beacons? (either the one that you entered or the one you exited at). I think this should be a thing, depending on how OP beacons are. When consumed, beacons leave behind a square fully in your color.
Optional (70/30): Beacons count +N to stamina regen wherever you are on the map. (N should be a very small amount to prevent beacon farming)
You can paint 1 square at a time around yourself (each tile holds a value anywhere from -3 to 3, where -3 is fully blue and 3 is fully green) 
Can’t paint underneath yourself
Each paint action increments that tile’s paint value negative (if blue) and positive (if green), you can only paint on your own color
Optional (50/50): you can only paint either behind you/ in the direction you are facing (snake directional idea)
Collisions
If your opponent collides with you on your own paint, you win
If your opponent collides with you on their paint, you lose
If you collide with your opponent on an unpainted square, whoever gets hit loses
Competitor would want to go back to their painted square costing stamina
Optional (20/80): Take average of paint around collision to decide instead of just the square collided on
Full diagonal pathing (k=8)
Could have standard grid pathing (k=4) or snake pathing (k=5) 
Wins determined by Code crash, Time out, Invalid move, Stamina loss, Number of squares painted in your color
